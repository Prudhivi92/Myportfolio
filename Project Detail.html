Revolutionizing & Redefining Experiences for agents

Agents face challenges accessing and comparing Medicare Advantage plans due to fragmented systems and manual processes. This inefficiency hampers their ability to deliver accurate, personalized recommendations, impacting both productivity and customer satisfaction.

Client

My Role

Responsive web design

Platform

Drive strategy, UX research, Analysis, wireframing, and visual design.

Uncovering Key Insights Through Interviews

As part of our strategic initiative to enhance user engagement and satisfaction, we conducted in-depth qualitative research with internal sales leadership and frontline agents. This research focused on gaining a deeper understanding of targeted user segments that align with overarching business goals.

Synthesize our key findings

After completing user interviews with agents, members and sales leaders we conducted an affinity mapping session where we documented each insight on sticky notes and grouped them into meaningful categories. This process helped us visualize recurring pain points specific to each group and align those insights with broader business goals in the health insurance space.

Competitor Analysis

To establish a baseline understanding of where UHC stands in the future roadmap, we analyzed UHC’s digital experience alongside two healthcare competitors and one best-in-class out-of-industry brand to benchmark UHC’s position in the market.

We concluded that within the industry, all competitors’ product portfolios are about equal, and there is room for UHC to create a user experience that is comparable the leading out of industry players.

Persona

Personas helped us to transform abstract research data into relatable user profiles for agents and members, enabling us to identify and refine the most effective ways to engage each user type across various touchpoints.

Transforming the Agent Experience Through a Seamless Omnichannel Strategy

Enhancing agent productivity through map integration

Empower agents with interactive map integration that allows them to visualize Medicare Advantage plan availability by location. Streamline plan searches, reduce decision time, and ensure the most relevant options are matched to each customer—faster, more accurately, and with less effort.

Intuitive List View Experience

Explore a curated list of Medicare Advantage plans in a list view, complete with personalized filters that display key data—like premiums, coverage options, and market insights—designed specifically for agent selected market to help agents choose the right plan.

Effortless Plan Exploration, Rooted in Cognitive Clarity and Simplicity

Enhancing user engagement by presenting Medicare Advantage plan details alongside with contextual content with a clear, structured layout—highlighting premiums, coverage, Ancillary benefits, service area etc with personalized recommendations, to guide users toward confident, well-informed decisions.

Make Medicare Decisions Easy by Comparing Plans

Quickly compare Medicare Advantage plans side by side—see premiums, coverage, doctors, and extra benefits all in one place. Make confident decisions with clear, personalized information, and use visual cues to highlight key differences. Save, share, or print your comparisons anytime for a seamless experience across devices.
